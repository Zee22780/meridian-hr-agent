# Benefits Overview

**Last Updated:** January 2026  
**Applies To:** All full-time employees

---

## Health Insurance

Meridian offers three health insurance tiers through BlueCross BlueShield:

- **Basic Plan** — Lower monthly premium, higher deductible ($1,500 individual / $3,000 family)
- **Standard Plan** — Balanced premium and deductible ($750 individual / $1,500 family)
- **Premium Plan** — Higher monthly premium, lowest deductible ($250 individual / $500 family)

**Eligibility:** Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance on their **first day of employment**. Part-time employees (fewer than 30 hours per week) are not eligible.

**Enrollment Window:** You must elect a plan within **30 days of your start date**. If you miss this window, you must wait until the next open enrollment period (each November) unless you experience a qualifying life event (marriage, birth of a child, loss of other coverage).

**Dependent Coverage:** Spouses, domestic partners, and dependent children up to age 26 are eligible for coverage. Dependent coverage requires documentation submitted to People Operations within 30 days.

**Premium Contributions:** Meridian covers 80% of the employee premium and 60% of dependent premiums. Your share is deducted pre-tax from each paycheck.

---

## Dental Insurance

Meridian provides dental coverage through Delta Dental.

- **Preventive care** (cleanings, X-rays): Covered at 100%
- **Basic restorative** (fillings, extractions): Covered at 80%
- **Major restorative** (crowns, root canals): Covered at 50%
- **Orthodontia:** Covered at 50%, lifetime maximum of $1,500 (adults and children)

**Eligibility:** Same as health insurance — first day of employment for full-time employees.

---

## Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is provided through VSP.

- Annual eye exam: Covered at 100% (in-network)
- Frames or contact lenses: Up to $150 allowance per year
- LASIK discount program available through VSP network providers

**Eligibility:** First day of employment for full-time employees.

---

## Life Insurance

Meridian provides basic life insurance equal to **1x your annual base salary** at no cost to you. You may purchase supplemental life insurance up to 5x your salary during open enrollment or within 30 days of hire.

---

## Short-Term and Long-Term Disability

- **Short-Term Disability (STD):** Covers 60% of your base salary for up to 12 weeks after a 7-day waiting period. Applies to non-work-related illness or injury.
- **Long-Term Disability (LTD):** Activates after STD is exhausted. Covers 60% of base salary up to age 65.

Both plans are company-paid. Employees in their first 90 days are not eligible for STD or LTD.

---

## Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

You may contribute pre-tax dollars to a Healthcare FSA (up to $3,200/year) or a Dependent Care FSA (up to $5,000/year). FSA elections must be made during open enrollment or within 30 days of hire.

**Note:** FSA funds are use-it-or-lose-it. Up to $640 may roll over to the following plan year; any remaining balance is forfeited.

---

## How to Enroll

Benefits enrollment is managed through the Meridian HR portal. You will receive a welcome email from People Operations with your login credentials and a step-by-step enrollment guide within your first two business days.

If you have questions about plan options or need help enrolling, contact the People Operations team at **people@meridian.com** or speak with your HR rep during your onboarding orientation.

---

## Questions and Edge Cases

- **Already have coverage through a spouse?** You may waive Meridian coverage. Waiver forms are available in the HR portal.
- **Changing plans mid-year?** Only permitted during open enrollment or after a qualifying life event.
- **Contractor or intern?** Benefits eligibility does not extend to contractors or interns regardless of hours worked. Contact People Operations if your employment status changes.
