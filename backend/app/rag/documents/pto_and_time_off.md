# PTO & Time Off Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026  
**Applies To:** All full-time employees

---

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Meridian uses a unified PTO bank — vacation, personal days, and sick days are drawn from the same balance. There is no separate sick day allocation.

### Accrual Rates

PTO accrues each pay period (bi-weekly) based on years of service:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO | Accrual per Pay Period |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 years | 15 days | 0.58 days |
| 1–3 years | 18 days | 0.69 days |
| 3–5 years | 21 days | 0.81 days |
| 5+ years | 25 days | 0.96 days |

**New hires begin accruing PTO on their first day.** PTO is not front-loaded — you accrue as you go.

### PTO Availability

You may use accrued PTO as soon as it appears in your balance. Negative PTO balances are not permitted except in cases of pre-approved extended leave (see below).

### Carryover

Up to **10 days** of unused PTO may be carried over into the following calendar year. Any balance above 10 days is forfeited on January 1. Employees are encouraged to use PTO throughout the year.

### PTO Payout at Separation

Accrued, unused PTO is paid out upon voluntary resignation or termination. PTO is not paid out if an employee is terminated for cause.

---

## Company Holidays

Meridian observes the following paid holidays each year:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Juneteenth
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve (half day)
- Christmas Day

When a holiday falls on a Saturday, the preceding Friday is observed. When it falls on a Sunday, the following Monday is observed.

**Floating Holiday:** Each employee receives one floating holiday per calendar year to use at their discretion. It must be used by December 31 and does not carry over.

---

## Requesting Time Off

Time off requests are submitted through the HR portal. General guidelines:

- **Less than 3 days:** Submit at least 3 business days in advance
- **3–5 days:** Submit at least 2 weeks in advance
- **More than 5 consecutive days:** Submit at least 4 weeks in advance

Requests are subject to manager approval. Approval is not guaranteed during blackout periods (see below).

### Blackout Periods

Certain high-demand periods may be designated as blackout periods by department heads, during which PTO requests may be denied. Blackout periods must be communicated to the team at least 30 days in advance. Blackout periods vary by department — contact your manager for your team's schedule.

---

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is drawn from the general PTO bank. There is no separate sick day allocation. You do not need manager approval to use PTO for same-day illness — notify your manager as early as possible and log the absence in the HR portal.

For absences of **3 or more consecutive days** due to illness, a doctor's note may be requested by People Operations.

---

## Bereavement Leave

Meridian provides paid bereavement leave separate from PTO:

- **Immediate family** (spouse, child, parent, sibling): Up to 5 paid days
- **Extended family** (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle): Up to 3 paid days
- **Other relationships:** Up to 1 paid day; additional time may be taken from PTO

Bereavement leave does not need to be taken consecutively. Notify your manager and People Operations as soon as possible.

---

## Parental Leave

- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks fully paid, beginning on the child's birth or adoption date
- **Secondary caregiver:** 6 weeks fully paid

Parental leave is available to all full-time employees who have completed **90 days of employment** at the time of the leave. Employees who have not met the 90-day threshold may request unpaid leave or use accrued PTO.

Parental leave runs concurrently with any state-mandated leave. Contact People Operations at least 60 days before the expected leave date to coordinate benefits continuation and return-to-work planning.

---

## Unpaid Leave of Absence

Extended unpaid leave may be requested for personal, medical, or family reasons beyond what is covered by PTO or parental leave. Requests must be submitted to People Operations and approved by your manager and department head. Benefits continue during approved unpaid leave for up to 90 days; after 90 days, COBRA continuation applies.

---

## Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees accrue PTO on a prorated basis relative to a 40-hour work week. For example, an employee working 20 hours per week accrues PTO at 50% of the full-time rate. Part-time employees are eligible for company holidays that fall on their scheduled work days.

---

## Questions

Contact People Operations at **people@meridian.com** or speak with your HR rep for questions about your PTO balance, leave requests, or special circumstances not covered here.
