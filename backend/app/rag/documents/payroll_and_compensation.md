# Payroll & Compensation

**Last Updated:** January 2026  
**Applies To:** All full-time and part-time employees

---

## Pay Schedule

Meridian pays employees on a **bi-weekly schedule** — every other Friday, for the two-week period ending the previous Sunday. There are 26 pay periods per year.

If a scheduled payday falls on a bank holiday, payment is issued on the preceding business day.

**New hires** are paid on the next regular payday following their start date, provided their onboarding paperwork (including direct deposit and W-4) is submitted at least **5 business days** before that payday. If paperwork is submitted late, the first paycheck may be delayed to the following pay period.

---

## Direct Deposit

Direct deposit is the standard payment method at Meridian. Employees must submit their banking information through the HR portal during onboarding. Up to **two accounts** may be designated — for example, a checking account for the majority of pay and a savings account for a fixed amount or percentage.

Paper checks are available upon request but may take up to 5 business days to arrive and are subject to a $5 processing fee per check.

---

## Understanding Your Paycheck

Each pay stub includes:

- **Gross pay** — total earnings before deductions
- **Pre-tax deductions** — health/dental/vision premiums, FSA contributions, 401(k) contributions
- **Federal and state taxes** — withheld based on your W-4 elections
- **Post-tax deductions** — supplemental life insurance, Roth 401(k) contributions (if elected)
- **Net pay** — the amount deposited to your account

Pay stubs are available in the HR portal within 24 hours of each payday. Physical copies are not mailed.

---

## Overtime

Meridian complies with the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) and applicable state laws.

- **Non-exempt employees** are eligible for overtime pay at **1.5x their regular hourly rate** for hours worked over 40 in a workweek. Overtime must be pre-approved by your manager. Working unapproved overtime does not exempt the company from paying it, but unauthorized overtime may result in disciplinary action.
- **Exempt employees** (salaried, meeting FLSA salary and duties thresholds) are not eligible for overtime pay. Most full-time salaried roles at Meridian are classified as exempt.

If you are unsure of your FLSA classification, it is listed in your offer letter and in the HR portal under your employee profile.

## Salary Reviews

Meridian conducts annual compensation reviews each **March**, aligned with the fiscal year. Factors considered include:

- Individual performance rating from the prior year
- Market compensation benchmarking (Meridian targets the 50th–75th percentile for its industry)
- Internal equity across the team
- Company financial performance

Salary adjustments resulting from annual reviews are effective **April 1**. There is no guaranteed raise — compensation adjustments are merit-based and subject to budget availability.

**New hires** are not eligible for a salary review in their first March if they started after **October 1** of the prior year. Their first review will occur in March of the following year.

## Bonuses

### Performance Bonus

Eligible employees may receive an annual performance bonus paid in **February**, based on the prior year's performance. Bonus eligibility and target percentages are outlined in your offer letter.

- Employees must be **actively employed on the bonus payment date** to receive it. Employees who resign or are terminated before the payment date forfeit the bonus, regardless of performance.
- Employees hired after **July 1** of the bonus year receive a prorated bonus based on months worked.

### Referral Bonus

Meridian pays a **$2,000 referral bonus** for successful employee referrals. The bonus is paid in two installments: $1,000 when the referred employee starts, and $1,000 after the referred employee completes 90 days. If the referred employee leaves before 90 days, the second installment is forfeited.

---

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses (travel, client meals, software subscriptions) are reimbursed within **10 business days** of submission through the HR portal. Receipts are required for all expenses over **$25**. Expenses must be submitted within **60 days** of being incurred — late submissions may be denied.

Expense limits by category:

- Meals (client-facing): Up to $75 per person
- Domestic travel: Coach class airfare, standard hotel room
- International travel: Requires VP approval before booking

For questions about tax withholding, consult your W-4 elections in the portal or speak with a personal tax advisor — Meridian cannot provide individual tax advice.
